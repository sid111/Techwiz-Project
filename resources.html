Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access online library resources?

You can access online library resources through the student portal using your college credentials. The library database includes e-books, academic journals, research papers, and other digital resources available 24/7.

Where can I find tutoring support?

The Academic Success Center offers free tutoring for most subjects. You can schedule appointments online through the student portal or drop in during their operating hours. Both in-person and online tutoring options are available.

How do I apply for financial aid?

To apply for financial aid, complete the FAFSA form online and use our school code. Additionally, check the scholarship portal for institution-specific scholarships and grants. The financial aid office offers workshops and one-on-one counseling to assist with the process.

What IT support is available for students?

The IT Help Desk provides support for technical issues including password resets, software installation, network connectivity, and online learning platforms. Support is available via phone, email, chat, and in-person at the technology center.
